A Sick Trick

Happy, a wonderful little kindergarten boy, is sad today. He has gotten tricked.
How has he gotten tricked? You are about to find that out now.
Earlier this day, he was at recess, at school, playing with the girls. One of the girls said to him...
"Go to the sandpit. There's a big suprise I left for you there!"
Happy was happy. It was not often he had heard those words. He raced to the sandpit.
He dug for five minutes when he found something. "A chocolate cake!" he exclaimed.
The girls snickered.
Happy picked it up, and put it close to his mouth.
Thre girls laughed.
Happy ate a piece.
The girls were hysterical.
